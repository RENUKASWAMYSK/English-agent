# 🌟 English Quest

**Learn English from Kannada — Candy Crush style!**

A free English learning game designed specifically for Kannada speakers who want to reach American English fluency.

## 🎮 Play Now
👉 **[Click here to play](https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/english-quest/)**

## 📱 Install on Phone
1. Open the link above in Chrome on your phone
2. Tap the menu (3 dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Now it works like a real app!

## 🌟 Features
- **200+ handcrafted levels** — from village Kannada English to American fluency
- **AI-generated infinite levels** — never runs out!
- **5 question types** — MCQ, Word Blast, Match Pairs, Rapid Fire, Voice Input
- **Full story** — follow Arjun/Priya from a Karnataka village to San Francisco
- **Candy Crush map** — locked levels, stars, checkpoints, boss battles
- **Daily challenges** — new challenge every day
- **Leaderboard** — compete with friends
- **Sound effects** — correct/wrong/combo/win sounds
- **Works offline** — no internet needed after first load
